Change of Location for Best Practices Working Group

Due to the overwhelming response, OIP has moved the location of the Best Practices Working Group to a larger venue that will accommodate more people. The details are set forth below.

When: Tuesday, June 15, 2010

Where:
National Housing Center
1201 15th Street NW
(entrance on 15th and M Street)

Time: 1:00 pm - 3:00 pm

Please register in advance for the Best Practices Working Group with OIP’s Training Officer, Bertina Adams Cleveland, at (202) 514-1010. (posted 05/19/2010)
