Referrals and Consultations to be topic of July 14 FOIA Requester Roundtable

During the FOIA Requester Roundtable held in May, the participants asked that the topic of document referrals and consultations be the focus of the next Roundtable. Based on that interest, we have switched the topics for the July 14, 2011, and January 12, 2012 Requester Roundtables. On July 14, 2011, the topic for discussion will now be “Document Referrals and Consultations,” and on January 12, 2012, the topic for discussion will be “Records on Third Parties.” To view details about participating in any of the upcoming FOIA Requester Roundtables, see http://www.justice.gov/oip/foiapost/2011foiapost08.html.
